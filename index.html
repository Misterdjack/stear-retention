About Us

STEAR Retention is designed to integrate offices across campus to build a holistic, total student focus. Our strategy is to build the best system to meet your retention goals. Retention strategies will vary from institution to institution, but the data behind your efforts will be consolidated and shared.

We listen and respond to your requests. We work with you and your staff to constantly improve our products and to help you reach your goals. We tie people, processes and technology together.
